A · Two small aggregates (recommended)
Engagement context
 ├─ XpAccount (aggregate)      keyed by LearnerId
 │    TotalXp, AppliedAwards[]
 └─ LearnerStreak (aggregate)  keyed by LearnerId
      Current, Longest,
      LastQualifyingDate, TimeZoneId

both subscribe to LessonCompleted independently
Each aggregate is small, single-purpose, independently testable and lockable.

The principle

An aggregate is a consistency boundary — group only what must change together to hold an invariant. XP and streak share no invariant (awarding XP doesn't constrain the streak count). So they should be separate aggregates, each keyed by LearnerId, each reacting to LessonCompleted on its own. This also showcases our choreography seam: two independent subscribers to one event — no orchestrator.
