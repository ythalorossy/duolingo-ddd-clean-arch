Streaks — Sub-project 2

Growing the Engagement core. We'll work through: what counts as a day → day boundary / timezone → state machine → streak freeze scope → data model & integration.

Q1 · What does it take to keep the streak alive for a day?

A "streak" = consecutive days of qualifying activity. But what makes a day qualify? Here are the two candidate rules on a 7-day timeline (✅ = day qualifies, ❌ = missed).

A · Any lesson completed
One LessonCompleted on a day = that day qualifies. 
Mon Tue Wed Thu Fri Sat Sun
 ✅  ✅  ✅  ❌  ✅  ✅  ✅
 1   2   3   0   1   2   3   ← streak
Simple. Streak counts "days you showed up at all."
B · Meet a daily goal
A day qualifies only if you hit a goal (e.g. ≥ 1 goal's worth of XP / N lessons). 
Mon Tue Wed Thu Fri Sat Sun
 ✅  ⚠️  ✅  ✅  ❌  ✅  ✅
 1   1   2   3   0   1   2   ← streak
(Tue: did a lesson but missed goal → ⚠️ no credit)
Richer: introduces a "daily goal" concept the streak depends on.
