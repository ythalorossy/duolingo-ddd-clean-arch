Thursday was missed → one freeze auto-applied → streak survived at 5 (it did not jump to 6). Friday's lesson then continues the chain to 6.

The rules baked into that picture
  • 🧊 A freeze is auto-applied the instant a gap is detected — the learner does nothing.
  • A frozen day preserves the streak but does not increment it (Thu stayed at 5).
  • Inventory is capped (assume max 2 for now) — you can't hoard infinite freezes.
  • Miss more consecutive days than you have freezes → the streak still breaks and resets.
  • No nightly job (consistent with streaks today): the freeze is settled lazily — when the next lesson arrives, or when the streak is read.
